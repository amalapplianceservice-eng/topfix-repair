Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your repair.

Do you offer same-day appliance repair in Los Angeles?

Yes. In most Los Angeles neighborhoods we offer same-day or next-day appointments. Call before noon and we'll do our best to have a technician at your door the same day.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Every TopFix Repair technician is factory-trained, fully licensed, background-checked, and insured, so your home or business is always protected.

Do I need to be home during the repair appointment?

An adult needs to be present to give our technician access and approve the work, but most repairs are quick — many are completed within about an hour of arrival.

Do you repair commercial appliances for restaurants and businesses?

Yes. We service commercial kitchens, laundromats, offices, and property managers across LA — including walk-in coolers, commercial ovens, ice machines, and stacked laundry systems — with priority response.

What warranty do you offer on repairs?

All TopFix repairs are backed by a 90-day labor warranty and up to a 1-year warranty on installed parts, giving you complete peace of mind.

Which areas of Los Angeles do you serve?

We serve all of Greater Los Angeles, including Downtown, Hollywood, Silver Lake, Santa Monica, Koreatown, Pasadena, the Valley, and surrounding communities.
