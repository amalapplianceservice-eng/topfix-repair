Common problems we fix

Hover over any issue below to see what may be causing it. Our factory-trained technicians arrive fully stocked to handle the most common dryer repair problems—usually in a single visit.

  • ✓ Dryer won't heat upHover for details

    Usually a blown thermal fuse, a failed heating element (electric) or igniter (gas), or a tripped thermostat caused by restricted airflow.

  • ✓ Takes too long to dry clothesHover for details

    Most often a clogged lint filter or vent, weak airflow, or a heating element working below full capacity.

  • ✓ Drum not turningHover for details

    Commonly a broken drive belt, a worn drum roller or idler pulley, or a failed drive motor.

  • ✓ Loud or squeaking noisesHover for details

    Typically worn drum rollers, a failing idler pulley, or a glide and bearing that need replacing.

  • ✓ Won't start or shuts off earlyHover for details

    Often a faulty door switch, thermal fuse, start switch, or an overheating sensor cutting the cycle short.

  • ✓ Overheating or burning smellHover for details

    A serious sign of heavy lint buildup, a blocked vent, or a malfunctioning thermostat. Shut it off and call us right away.

Good to know

Dryer Repair FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about dryer repair in Los Angeles.

Are your dryer technicians qualified to work on gas appliances?

Yes. Our technicians are fully licensed and trained to safely service both gas and electric dryers, including the gas connections that electric-only handymen are not permitted to touch.

Is a dryer that won't heat worth repairing?

Almost always yes — a no-heat issue is usually a straightforward element, fuse, or igniter rather than a major failure. We will tell you honestly if a replacement ever makes more sense.

My dryer takes forever to dry — is that dangerous?

It can be. Long dry times often mean a clogged vent, which is also a fire risk. We diagnose the airflow, clear the problem, and make your dryer safe and efficient again.

Do you repair both gas and electric dryers?

Yes. Our technicians are trained and licensed to safely repair both gas and electric dryers from all major brands.

Is the repair covered by a warranty?

Every dryer repair comes with a 90-day labor warranty and up to a 1-year warranty on installed parts for complete peace of mind.
