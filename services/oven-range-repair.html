Common problems we fix

Hover over any issue below to see what may be causing it. Our factory-trained technicians arrive fully stocked to handle the most common oven & range repair problems–usually in a single visit.

  • ✓ Oven not heating or uneven heatHover for details

    Usually a failed bake or broil element, a faulty igniter (gas), or a temperature sensor giving the control board wrong readings.

  • ✓ Burner won't ignite or stay litHover for details

    Common causes are a dirty or worn igniter, a faulty spark module, or a clogged burner port restricting gas flow.

  • ✓ Inaccurate temperatureHover for details

    Often a failing oven sensor or a control board that needs recalibration so your dishes cook evenly every time.

  • ✓ Faulty control board or knobsHover for details

    Unresponsive controls usually mean a failed electronic control board, worn selector switches, or damaged wiring.

  • ✓ Self-clean cycle not workingHover for details

    Typically a faulty door lock motor, a thermal fuse blown by the high heat, or a control board fault.

  • ✓ Gas smell or ignition clickingHover for details

    Never ignore a gas smell. Constant clicking or odor points to a misaligned igniter or a gas valve issue–we'll make it safe fast.

Good to know

Oven & Range Repair FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about oven & range repair in Los Angeles.

Are your technicians certified to repair gas ovens and ranges?

Yes. Our technicians are licensed and trained to safely service gas, electric, and dual-fuel ovens and ranges, including all gas components.

Is it safe to use my oven with a gas smell or constant clicking?

No – stop using it and call us right away. A gas smell or non-stop clicking points to an igniter or gas valve issue we will make safe quickly. Your safety comes first.

Should I repair my oven or replace it?

Most oven problems – bad elements, igniters, or sensors – are well worth repairing. We give you an honest call and never recommend a needless replacement.

Do you repair gas, electric, and dual-fuel ranges?

Yes. We service gas, electric, and dual-fuel ovens and ranges from every major brand, including Wolf, Viking, Samsung, LG, GE, Bosch and KitchenAid.

Is the repair guaranteed?

Yes – a 90-day labor warranty (and we install only original parts) backs every oven and range repair we perform.
