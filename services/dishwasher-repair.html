Common problems we fix

Hover over any issue below to see what may be causing it. Our factory-trained technicians arrive fully stocked to handle the most common dishwasher repair problems—usually in a single visit.

  • ✓ Dishes not getting cleanHover for details

    Often clogged spray arms, a worn wash pump, a failing soap dispenser, or water entering the unit at too low a temperature.

  • ✓ Won't drain or standing waterHover for details

    Usually a clogged drain hose or filter, a failed drain pump, or a blocked garbage-disposal connection.

  • ✓ Leaking onto the floorHover for details

    Common causes are a worn door gasket, a cracked hose, a loose connection, or a faulty float switch overfilling the tub.

  • ✓ Won't start or fillHover for details

    Typically a faulty door latch, a failed water inlet valve, or a control board not initiating the cycle.

  • ✓ Soap dispenser not openingHover for details

    Often a broken dispenser latch, a worn wax motor, or hardened detergent residue jamming the door.

  • ✓ Strange noises during the cycleHover for details

    Usually a worn wash-pump or motor bearing, a spray arm striking dishes, or debris caught in the chopper assembly.

Dishwasher Repair FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about dishwasher repair in Los Angeles.

How long does a dishwasher repair take?

Most dishwasher repairs are completed in a single visit, usually within an hour or two, since our technicians arrive with the most common parts on board.

Is it worth repairing a dishwasher that won't clean or drain?

Usually yes — poor cleaning and drainage issues are typically straightforward pump, spray-arm, or valve fixes. We give you an honest assessment before any work begins.

Can a leaking dishwasher damage my floor — how fast can you come?

A leak can absolutely damage cabinets and flooring, so we treat it as a priority. In most LA areas we offer same-day or next-day appointments to stop the leak fast.

Do you repair all dishwasher brands?

Yes — Bosch, Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, KitchenAid, GE, Maytag and more, including built-in and panel-ready models.

What warranty do you offer?

Every dishwasher repair includes a 90-day labor warranty and up to a 1-year warranty on installed parts.
