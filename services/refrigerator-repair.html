Common problems we fix

Hover over any issue below to see what may be causing it. Our factory-trained technicians arrive fully stocked to handle the most common refrigerator repair problems–usually in a single visit.

  • ✓ Refrigerator not cooling or freezingHover for details

    Often caused by a faulty compressor, dirty or blocked condenser coils, a failing start relay, or a thermostat that's no longer reading the temperature correctly.

  • ✓ Water leaking onto the floorHover for details

    Usually a clogged or frozen defrost drain, a damaged water inlet valve, or a cracked drain pan. We trace the source and seal it for good.

  • ✓ Ice maker not workingHover for details

    Common culprits are a frozen water line, a failed inlet valve, a stuck ejector motor, or a freezer that isn't cold enough to form ice.

  • ✓ Compressor or fan running loudlyHover for details

    A noisy unit often points to a worn evaporator or condenser fan motor, debris caught in the fan blades, or a compressor working under strain.

  • ✓ Frost buildup in the freezerHover for details

    Typically a failing defrost heater or timer, or a worn door gasket letting warm, humid air sneak inside and refreeze.

  • ✓ Temperature won't hold steadyHover for details

    Frequently a faulty thermostat, dirty condenser coils, or a control board that needs recalibration or replacement.

Refrigerator Repair FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about refrigerator repair in Los Angeles.

Are your refrigerator technicians licensed and insured?

Yes. Every TopFix technician is factory-trained, fully licensed, background-checked, and insured, so your home and your appliance are always in safe hands.

Is it worth repairing my fridge or should I just replace it?

If your refrigerator is under 8–10 years old, a repair is almost always the smarter choice. We give you an honest assessment, and if a repair truly does not make sense we will tell you straight rather than upsell you.

My fridge stopped cooling – can you come the same day?

In most LA neighborhoods, yes. Our vans are stocked with the most common parts, so we finish the majority of refrigerator repairs in a single same-day or next-day visit to save your groceries.

Do you repair built-in and high-end refrigerators?

Absolutely. We service all brands including Sub-Zero, Viking, Bosch, Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE and KitchenAid – both standard and built-in models.

Is the repair guaranteed?

Yes. Every refrigerator repair is backed by our 90-day labor warranty (and we install only original parts), so you are fully protected.
