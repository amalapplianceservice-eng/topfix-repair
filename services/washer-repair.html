Common problems we fix

Hover over any issue below to see what may be causing it. Our factory-trained technicians arrive fully stocked to handle the most common washer repair problems—usually in a single visit.

  • ✓ Washer won't spin or agitateHover for details

    Often a worn drive belt, a failed lid switch or door lock, a broken motor coupler, or a clutch that needs replacing.

  • ✓ Won't drain or fill with waterHover for details

    Usually a clogged drain pump or hose, a faulty water inlet valve, or a failed pressure switch that controls water levels.

  • ✓ Leaking during the cycleHover for details

    Common causes include a cracked hose, a worn door boot seal, a loose pump connection, or simply an overloaded drum.

  • ✓ Error codes on the displayHover for details

    Error codes point to a specific fault—from drainage and balance issues to control board or sensor failures. We read and clear them properly.

  • ✓ Excessive shaking or noiseHover for details

    Often worn shock absorbers or suspension springs, failing drum bearings, or an unbalanced load stressing the machine.

  • ✓ Door or lid won't lockHover for details

    Typically a failed lid switch, a faulty door latch assembly, or a control board not sending the lock signal.

Washer Repair FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about washer repair in Los Angeles.

Do I need to be home during the washer repair?

An adult does need to be present to give our technician access and approve the work, but the visit is quick — most washer repairs are completed in about an hour.

Should I repair my washer or buy a new one?

If your washer is under 8 years old, repairing it is usually the smarter choice. We give you an honest recommendation and will never push a repair that does not make sense for your machine.

Can you fix my washer the same day?

In most cases, yes. Our technicians carry common washer parts, so problems like no-spin, no-drain, and leaks are often solved in one same-day or next-day visit.

Do you repair both front-load and top-load washers?

Yes — we repair all front-load, top-load, and stackable washers from every major brand, including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Maytag, GE and Bosch.

What if the same problem comes back?

It is covered. Every washer repair includes a 90-day labor warranty and up to a 1-year parts warranty, so a repeat issue on the same repair is fully covered.
