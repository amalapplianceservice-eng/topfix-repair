Common problems we fix

Hover over any issue below to see what may be causing it. Our factory-trained technicians arrive fully stocked to handle the most common freezer repair problems–usually in a single visit.

  • ✓ Freezer not freezingHover for details

    Often a failing compressor, blocked condenser coils, a faulty start relay, or a thermostat reading the temperature incorrectly.

  • ✓ Excessive frost or ice buildupHover for details

    Typically a failing defrost heater or timer, or a worn door gasket letting humid air inside to refreeze.

  • ✓ Running constantly or too warmHover for details

    Usually dirty condenser coils, a worn door seal, or a thermostat that won't cycle the compressor off.

  • ✓ Leaking waterHover for details

    Commonly a clogged or frozen defrost drain, or a cracked drain pan that we'll clear and reseal.

  • ✓ Loud compressor or fan noiseHover for details

    Often a worn evaporator or condenser fan motor, debris in the blades, or a compressor under strain.

  • ✓ Door seal not closingHover for details

    A worn or warped gasket lets cold escape and frost form–we replace it to restore an airtight seal.

Good to know

Freezer Repair FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about freezer repair in Los Angeles.

Do you repair commercial freezers as well as home units?

Yes. We service residential stand-alone and built-in freezers along with light commercial units for restaurants and businesses across LA.

My freezer is failing – how fast can you protect my food?

We treat thawing freezers as urgent. In most LA neighborhoods we offer same-day or next-day service so you do not lose your frozen inventory.

Is it worth repairing my freezer or replacing it?

If your freezer is under 8–10 years old, a repair is almost always the smarter choice. We give you an honest assessment and never push an unnecessary replacement.

Do you repair stand-alone and built-in freezers?

Yes – chest, upright, drawer, and built-in freezers from all major brands, including Sub-Zero, Frigidaire, Samsung, LG, GE and Whirlpool.

What warranty comes with the repair?

Every freezer repair is backed by a 90-day labor warranty (and we install only original parts).
